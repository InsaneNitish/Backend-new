Duroflex Mattress – Sleep Like Never Before


About Duroflex

Duroflex is India’s leading sleep solutions provider, offering a wide range of high-quality
mattresses designed for ultimate comfort and support. With over five decades of expertise,
Duroflex is committed to innovation, using advanced sleep technology to improve the way India sleeps.


Why Choose Duroflex?

✅ Scientifically Engineered for Better Sleep – Our mattresses are backed by extensive
research to provide optimal spinal alignment and pressure relief.

✅ Premium Quality Materials – Made with high-density foam, natural latex,

and premium fabric covers for durability and comfort.

✅ Wide Range for Every Sleeper – Whether you prefer memory foam, orthopedic, or
spring mattresses, we have the perfect fit for your sleep needs.

✅ Doctor-Recommended Orthopedic Range – Our Duropedic collection is tested and
recommended by doctors for superior back support.

✅ Eco-Friendly & Safe – Crafted with non-toxic materials and eco-conscious manufacturing processes.

Our Mattress Collections


1. Duropedic – Orthopedic Mattress


Designed with advanced 5-zone orthopedic support for spine health, certified by doctors.
Perfect for those who need extra back support.

2. Energise – Performance Mattress


Infused with cooling gel technology and responsive foam layers, ensuring uninterrupted sleep for active lifestyles.


3. Natural Living – Latex Mattress


Crafted with 100% natural latex for eco-conscious comfort. Provides a plush feel with excellent breathability.

4. Essential – Everyday Comfort

High-quality and budget-friendly mattresses designed for daily comfort and long-lasting durability.
