Types of Wood Flooring


1.Solid Hardwood

Made from a single piece of wood. Long-lasting and can be sanded and refinished multiple times. Popular wood species: Oak, Maple, Cherry.


2.Engineered Wood

Composed of multiple layers of wood for added stability. Ideal for moisture-prone areas like basements. Offers the same elegant look as solid wood at a more affordable price.


3.Reclaimed Wood

Eco-friendly and sustainable option. Brings history and unique character to your home.
