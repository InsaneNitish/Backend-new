Restolex Mattresses


Restolex Mattress – Sleep in Comfort, Wake Up Refreshed


Why Choose Restolex?

At Restolex, we believe that quality sleep is the foundation of a healthy and happy life.
Our mattresses are designed with advanced sleep technology, ensuring maximum comfort, durability,
and support. With years of expertise in crafting high-quality sleep solutions, Restolex offers a range
of mattresses tailored to your needs.



Our Mattress Collection


1. Orthopedic Mattresses

Designed for those who need extra back support, our orthopedic mattresses help maintain spinal alignment
and relieve pressure points, ensuring a restful night’s sleep.



2. Memory Foam Mattresses

Experience luxury and body-contouring comfort with our premium memory foam mattresses.
These adapt to your body shape, reducing motion transfer and enhancing deep sleep.



3. Spring Mattresses

Crafted with high-quality pocketed or Bonnell springs, our spring mattresses provide
excellent breathability, bounce, and durability for a comfortable sleeping experience.



4. Latex Mattresses

For those who prefer a natural sleeping surface, our latex mattresses offer
hypoallergenic properties, breathability, and exceptional support.



5. Hybrid Mattresses

A perfect blend of foam and spring technology, our hybrid mattresses offer
superior support and comfort, catering to various sleeping preferences.
